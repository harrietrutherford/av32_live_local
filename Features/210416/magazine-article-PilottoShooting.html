DESIGNER FOCUS: PETER PILOTTO

Print pioneers Peter Pilotto and Christopher De Vos bring their exuberant styles to life with bold clashes of colours and textures.

 
 

The duo is renowned for the experimental-but-feminine embellishment that the layered lace skirt and vivid ruching exemplifies.

 


PETER PILOTTO White Lace Striped Gaze Dress

 
 

This stripy number is a masterclass in asymmetry, with the lines diagonally aligned from the single sleeve to the tiered skirt.


PETER PILOTTO Navy Lace Single-Shoulder Dress

 
 

Here it is, proof that smocking can be sexy when streamlined and ruffled to waist-cinching, hip-hugging effect. The pastel blue hue gives the sexy silhouette a girlish charm.

 

PETER PILOTTO Blue Ruched Knit Fitted Dress

 

A punchy kaleidoscopic print and directional mullet hemline transform a pink dress from pretty to progressive.

 

PETER PILOTTO Pink Silk Jacquard Sleeveless Dress

 
 
 

This crisp off-the-shoulder style is classic Bardot; statement stitching adds a modern edge.

 

PETER PILOTTO Stage Cotton Ballon Petra Dress
